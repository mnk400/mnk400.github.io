iPod Playlist

This interactive iPod is built using HTML, CSS, and JS to play some of my favorite songs. It's designed to mimic the very first iPod classic.

To interact, use the buttons on the iPod as you would on a real iPod. You can also use the keyboard: 'Up' and 'Down' arrows for navigation, 'Enter' to select or exit, and 'Space' for play/pause.

The music is streamed from YouTube.
