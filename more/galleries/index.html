All of this art is public domain, but most archives that host it are either wiki-style with rough navigation, museum pages with limited set of artwork, or buried with ads.

These pages are an attempt at a cleaner, searchable, sortable, filterable alternative. Started with Monet because nothing like it seemed to exist for him, then grew to cover a handful of other artists I like.
