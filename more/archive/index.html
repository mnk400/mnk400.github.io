A small browser for public-domain paintings and artwork from artists I like, each archive is searchable, sortable, and filterable.

{%- capture motivation -%}

All of this art is public domain, but most archives that host it are either wiki-style with rough navigation, museum pages with a limited set of artwork, or buried under ads.

These pages are an attempt at a cleaner alternative. Started with Monet because nothing like it seemed to exist for him, then grew to cover a handful of other artists I like.
